Frequently Asked Questions

We are a service dedicated to largely succulents and cacti, with a primary focus on easy-to-grow plants that would be well-suited for beginners. Browsing our options, you may find a variety of potted houseplants, i.e. Crassula, Echeveria or Astrophytum, Ferocactus and many more.

To place an order through our website, simply go to the web page of your desired product, specify the number of products you want to make a purchase (our products often come under one plant or one seed packet each), then press the “Add to Cart” button. After finishing browsing, you can check your cart by pressing the button on the upper right corner, then proceed to checkout after a confirmation. You will be presented with two options: to either pay beforehand through an online banking service of your choice from our available pool, or to pay after the product has been delivered successfully. Details of your address will also be registered with your account, which you are free to change at any time.

Bulk and wholesale purchases are available for our services. Customers looking to purchase our products in large batches will be given discounts proportional to the amount and cost of the final payment. In case you are interested in purchasing for your business, however, we would suggest contacting our customer service at alittlegreener@miniwonder.com to best handle the order. Aside from bulk discounts, we also offer sets of multiple products and seasonal sales promotions. To keep up with our latest releases and promotional campaigns, you can subscribe to our email service here or through your Account web page.

For instructions and tips on how to best take care of your new plants, including guides on water, light, soil, and other related matters, we would suggest you visit the web page of the specific product to best meet your needs.

Our shipping service is available all-year round, including during holidays. For more details on the service, please visit our details page here.

Our plants will be potted in a durable pot beforehand, and when an order is placed, we will secure the pot in a bubble wrap layer before placing them in a cardboard box with protective packing peanuts. The box will then be sealed with strong packing tape with holes on the top and sides to allow air and light to reach the plant.

Should your plant arrive damaged, please contact our Customer Service through our hotline 0923-08-37-12 or through our email alittlegreener@miniwonder.com to best meet your needs. We will provide a refund proportional to the damage your plants received during the shipping process.

Our website accepts online payment through Venmo, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay among other payment methods, as well as offers the option to pay in cash on delivery. For more details please visit our details page here.

You can track your order through your Account page on our website. Details on what step of the shipping process will be detailed.

If the product you received is faulty or damaged on arrival, or if the plant fail to survive within the first 10 days, you will be refunded proportional to the damage you receive, or you will be presented the choice to return the product completely, after which we will send a new product to you.

Most plants available for purchase on our website are non-toxic to pets and humans alike, though cacti may harm some animals with their spikes. For details on their potential toxicity, please visit the respective product page for more information.

Our plants collection is selected ideally for indoor planting and potting, but they would be able to grow outdoors as well. For more information, please visit the respective product page.

Our plants are raised with utmost care from professionals in the field, though as characteristic of all living things, sudden, unpredictable changes may occur in our products. Should the plants you ordered fail to survive within the first 10 days after arrival, we offer a return and refund policy.

Customers can choose to purchase our products as gifts for others, and can request gift wrapping or leave special messages at checkout by choosing the option “Purchase as gift”, after which additional information will be presented including the address they want to send to, messages to send, as well as the ability to choose the wrapping pattern to their liking from an available pool.

The growth size and reproductive rate of plants are highly specific to each genuses, and we suggest visiting the respective plant page for more information.

The reaction of plants to changing climates and weather is highly specific to each genuses, and we suggest visiting the respective plant page for more information.
